Lasagna

Description

Home made lasagna with fresh vegetables. A easy to do recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup onion
  • 4 gloves garlic
  • 2 can tomatoes
  • 12 ounces tomato paste
  • 13 ounces tomato souce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon with sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces cottage cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 pound mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. In a Dutch oven, over medium heat, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic until ground beef is cooked through and onions are translucent.
  2. Stir in tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water. Mix well.
  3. Add sugar, basil, Italian seasoning, 1 teaspoon salt, pepper, and 1 Tablespoons parsley. Cover and simmer for 1 hour, stirring regularly.
  4. In a separate mixing bowl, combine cottage cheese with egg, 1 Tablespoon parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  5. Preheat oven to 350*F.
  6. In a 9 x 13 casserole place 1 cup of tomato sauce on the bottom of the pan. Then layer lasagna noodle, cottage cheese mixture & tomato mixture. Top with a comfortable amount of the Mozzarella & Parmesan cheese then repeat the layers again, ending with the cheese.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  8. Remove foil and bake for additional 25 minutes.
  9. Remove from oven and serve immediately!
