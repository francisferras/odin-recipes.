Strawberrie and cream Bruschetta

Introduction

These Strawberries & Cream Bruschetta’s are quick to toss together and perfect for any party! Weddings, baby showers, quick dinner appetizer, it’s great for all the above!

Ingredients

  • 1 long French Baguette
  • 4 Tablespoons butter
  • 4 Tablespoons cinnamon sugar
  • 4 ounces (1/2 cup) cream cheese
  • 2 cups Strawberries sliced or diced
  • 2 Tablespoons powdered sugar

Steps

  1. Slice baguette into bite size rounds.
  2. Butter each round then sprinkle with cinnamon sugar.
  3. Put pan in oven and turn oven on to broil. WATCH CLOSELY as you just want the butter to melt and a hint of golden sides.
  4. Remove from oven and set aside.
  5. Mix cream cheese until smooth and creamy.
  6. Spread over each baguette round.
  7. In a small bowl combine strawberries and powdered sugar.
  8. Scoop strawberries on top of baguette rounds and devour!
